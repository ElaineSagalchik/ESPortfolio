Thank You Cards

Made with Figma

PROBLEM

Five clients wanted 4" x 6" thank you cards for separate occassions. One client wanted a thank you card the size of a standard business card, 3.5" x 2".

REQUIREMENTS

• Sit down with the client and understand their wants and requirements for the aesthetic and the occassion

• Mock-up 3-5 cards and present them to the client

• Iterate on the chosen version(s) until the client is satisfied with the design

SOLUTION OVERVIEW

• Every client received a design they were happy with.

• The client sent it to a printing shop/service of their choice
